CUSA Carnival

The CUSA Carnival was an original idea I came up with going into my second year as the Clarkson University Student Association's Public Relations Coordinator. The idea originates following a year of solitude due to the Covid 19 Pandemic. I wanted an event to bring the Clarkson University Students back together and uplift the Student Association! Planning this event following the pandemic was not an easy task, however with perserverence, I pushed thorough and ended up with an excellent display of Clarkson University Fun! The first year I managed the event primarily myself with a small committee of 3 other students. We incorperated over 20 clubs and organization from across campus, over 5 different external vendors, over 20 different professional departments across campus, and managed all of the set up and tear down of the event. We had an attendance of over 1500 students and the event was publicized by university and local media. The event was a huge sucess and was requested to become an annual event!

The second annual CUSA Carnival took place in august of 2022. Due to the sucerss of the first annual occurance, I was granted more resources resulting in an eased overall experience. None the less, We still incorperated numerous clubs, organizations, departments and had even more attendance than the first occurance. Every aspect of the event grew in size and I gained experience managing over 50 student staff and volunteer in event set up, tear down, and event processes. Following this event I created a guide book and the role in which I hold has been adapted to include running the CUSA Carnival as a part of the job description. I am actively training students in the student association on the procedural processes required for this event and I am extremely fortunate to say that THE CUSA Carnival is here at Clarkson to stay!

  • Organization : Clarkson University Student Association
  • Event Date : August 28th 2021 & August 29th 2022
  • Planning Period : 6-12 Months
