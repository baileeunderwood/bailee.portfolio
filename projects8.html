Bae Typography

This font, Bae Typography was created throughout my Typography and Design Course.

My inspiration for this font included Sage Serif, Kuta Serif, and Zanna Typefaces. I aimed for a unique font which stretched the boundaires of traditional typefaces. Here I encorperated a drastic extrema of think and thin strokes, as well as a edgy balance of simplistic and elaborate design features.
